How It Works

Our predictive maintenance system uses a Random Forest model trained on historical equipment data to predict when maintenance will be required. The system takes into account various parameters such as:

  • Air Temperature
  • Process Temperature
  • Rotational Speed
  • Torque
  • Tool Wear

By analyzing these parameters, our system can predict the condition of your equipment and provide recommendations for maintenance scheduling.
